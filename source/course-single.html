About the Course

This photography course is designed for both beginners and enthusiasts, providing a solid foundation in camera techniques, lighting, and post-processing. Whether you’re aspiring to go pro or just passionate about capturing life’s moments, you’ll learn practical skills and artistic concepts to improve your shots dramatically.

Requirements

  • Basic understanding of photography concepts is helpful.
  • Access to a DSLR or mirrorless camera is recommended.
  • Willingness to participate in field assignments.
  • Enthusiasm and curiosity for learning visual storytelling.
  • Regular attendance in practical workshops.
  • Internet access for submitting assignments online.
  • Basic photo editing software knowledge is a plus.
  • Open mindset to receive and implement feedback.

How to Apply

  • Fill out the online application form on our website.
  • Submit a brief personal statement explaining your interest.
  • Upload any photography samples (optional).
  • Pay the initial registration fee to secure your spot.
  • Check your email for confirmation and course start details.

Fees and Funding

The course fee includes access to all course materials, expert guidance, and participation in exhibitions and photo walks. Scholarships and installment plans are available for eligible students. Contact us to learn more about funding opportunities.
